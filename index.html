Sol Badguy

Sol Badguy is the main protagonist of the Guilty Gear series and Ky Kiske's rival. He is an aloof and brash bounty hunter of immense skill on a quest to find and eliminate all Gears and their creator, That Man. For a short time, Sol was also a member of the Sacred Order of Holy Knights and fought in the Crusades. This past version of him is known as "Order-Sol".

Dizzy

Dizzy is one of the main characters of the Guilty Gear series. An innocent and pacifist Commander-Type half-Gear, Dizzy is the daughter of Justice, who once waged war on humanity, but grew up unaware of her origins. The names of Dizzy's shapeshifting wings, also called her systems, are Necro, the Grim Reaper-like wing, and Undine, the blue angel wing.

Ky Kiske

Ky Kiske is the deuteragonist of the Guilty Gear series and Sol Badguy's rival. A former member of the Sacred Order of Holy Knights during the Crusades, Ky is a very charismatic swordsman who possesses a strong sense of justice and a devout religiosity, which sustain and guide him through all manner of shaking uncertainties.
